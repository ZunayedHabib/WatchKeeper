PC · .zip
WatchKeeper for PC
For Brave, Chrome, Edge and any Chromium browser on Windows, Mac or Linux. Extract the zip, then go to chrome://extensions → enable Developer Mode → click Load unpacked → select the extracted folder.
Download .zip
Android · .xpi
WatchKeeper for Android
For Quetta Browser on Android. Download this file to your phone, then in Quetta go to ⋮ → Settings → Extensions → Developer Mode → Load from .zip/.crx/.user.js and select the file.

How it works

Simple by design

WatchKeeper runs quietly in the background — no buttons to press, no manual saving.

1
Add your sites
Tell WatchKeeper which streaming sites to watch. It ignores everything else.
2
Watch normally
Your position is saved every 4 seconds automatically. No interruptions.
3
Resume anywhere
Return to any video on any device — WatchKeeper jumps you straight back in.
4
Everything syncs
Your data lives in your own Supabase database. Switch devices seamlessly.
5
Pin to top
Pin any video to always appear first in your list across sessions and devices.
6
Toggle sites
Pause tracking on any site temporarily with the ON / OFF toggle — no need to remove it.
